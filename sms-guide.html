SMS Booking Guide

FarmConnect allows you to book machinery and labour via SMS. Send your booking SMS to FARMCON (+1 7402674969).

Quick Tip: Copy any command below and send it as an SMS message. 24/7 Service

Machinery Booking

  • Format: BOOK [MACHINERY] [DATE] [LOCATION]
  • Example: BOOK TRACTOR 2026-03-15 KALYANI

Labour Booking

  • Format: LABOR [SKILL] [QUANTITY] [DATE]
  • Example: LABOR HARVESTING 5 2026-03-20

Supported Machinery Types

  • TRACTOR
  • HARVESTER
  • PLOUGH
  • CULTIVATOR
  • IRRIGATION
  • SPRAYER
  • DRONE

Supported Labour Skills

  • HARVESTING
  • PLANTING
  • IRRIGATION
  • SPRAYING
  • TRACTOR
  • ORGANIC

Confirmation

After sending your SMS, you will receive a confirmation reply with booking details and contact info.

Help

Send HELP to receive instructions via SMS.
